The traveling culture in Marysville is similar to the traveling culture in the rest of the United States. Cars are used everywhere and there is no infrastructure for public transit other than the school buses Marysville provides to their students. What can we do?

Things Marysville can do is specifically implement the basis for a public transport system, but since that takes a lot of money and time and brings Marysville into urbanization too fast, citizens can help bring down car usage. Below is a list of things we can do.

  • Use bikes to get around to places, great exercise.
  • Carpool with friends. This will bring down emissions of greenhouse gases and will reduce traffic by removing cars from the road
  • Choose fuel-efficient vehicles
