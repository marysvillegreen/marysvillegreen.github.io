3,683

TERA JOULES

What is that number?

According to our sources, Marysville consumed up to 3,683 tera joules. This powered 5,667 homes and several other businesses. Dayton Power & Light, Union Rural Electric Coop, Inc., are the Utility Companies that provide service to Marysville and they power most of the residences. Powering our homes can lead to global warming, learn about them below.


The terajoule (TJ) is equal to one trillion joules, according to the graph we use about 3,700 terajoules. In Marysville, the main industrial companies are Honda and Scotts Miracle-Gro. And that sector took up a majority of our energy spending. There was no data provided for Municipal buildings and Agriculture.








In 2015 Marysville used 41% of Gaseous Fuels and 29% of liquid fuels, this is a problem because this is a contributing factor to greenhouse gas emissions in Marysville and around the world. We can reduce this by implementing solar panels and renewal methods of powering Marysville.

T&D losses represent electricity that is generated but does not reach intended customers. Electricity losses are the result of technical inefficiency and theft. Technical losses occur because of the resistance of wires and equipment as electricity passes through. This is a problem as it is a wastage of electricity, Fortunatly, it's not a problem in Marysville as we only had a 4.7% loss
