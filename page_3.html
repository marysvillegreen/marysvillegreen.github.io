The Problem:

Greenhouse gases are a collection of gases in the atmosphere. They essentially harm our earth by warming the earth. Greenhouse gas molecules act like a mirror and they essentially send the heat we produce back to us, thereby heating up the earth. This has disastrous effects on all life on earth as it will increase the amount of droughts and other weather events. In 2015, Marysville produced 312, 238 tons of co2 and that number may have gone up since then.


Statistics:



Based on the data from 2015, Buildings in marysville took up most of emissions by 71%. All types of buildings contribute to the emissions especially in the residence subsectors and industrial subsectors. Currently there are no known instances of fugitive emissions.

Live Statistics:

Overall Air quality Index-

Overall Air quality measures of pollutants in our air. Our Live tracker is currently displaying Marysville Air Quality and it refreshes every 3 minutes. The range on this is 0 to 500, being that 0 is the best and 500 is the worst. Historically, Marysville has had a fine Air Quality measurement.

PM10 value- µg/m³

Pm10 stands for Particulate Matter, the value 10 is used as an indicator saying that the particulate has to be smaller than 10 micrometers. These particulates are formed from Agricultural sites, Construction, and Industrial sources. These particulates can penetrate our lungs and is generally bad for humans to inhale.

Carbon Monoxide-

Carbon Monoxide is a very dangerous thing as it is odorless and harmful to the human body. It is formed when combustible gases haven't been combusted and will replace oxygen molecules in our blood and potentially kill us. The Live Stat is listed right here and all information is gathered using an API

Nitrogen Dioxide-

Nitrogen Dioxide is bad for the environment and our health, once in contact with vegetation, it can decrease crop yield and growth. Health-wise, Nitrogen Dioxide can increase lung-related risks and illnesses such as asthma or various different respiratory infections. The Live Stat for Marysville, Ohio are listed here. All current info is used by API.
